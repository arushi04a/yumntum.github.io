Tiramisu Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mascarpone cheese
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 24 ladyfingers
  • 1 cup brewed coffee, cooled
  • 1/4 cup coffee liqueur (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, beat mascarpone cheese, sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth.
  2. In another bowl, whip heavy cream until stiff peaks form. Gently fold whipped cream into mascarpone mixture until well combined.
  3. In a shallow dish, combine brewed coffee and coffee liqueur (if using).
  4. Dip ladyfingers into coffee mixture briefly, ensuring they are not soaked through.
  5. Arrange a layer of dipped ladyfingers in the bottom of a serving dish.
  6. Spread half of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers.
  7. Repeat layers with remaining ladyfingers and mascarpone mixture.
  8. Cover and refrigerate tiramisu for at least 4 hours, or overnight, to set.
  9. Before serving, dust the top of tiramisu with cocoa powder.
